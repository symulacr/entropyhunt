## Vision

Entropy Hunt deploys autonomous drone swarms over disaster terrain for search-and-rescue missions. Drones partition terrain via Voronoi cells, resolve zone conflicts through Byzantine-fault-tolerant consensus over a peer-to-peer mesh, and emit cryptographically signed coordination proofs. The swarm continues operating when up to 30% of agents drop out — every decision is voted on, signed, and auditable.

Built with Python (core engine + inline TUI), Rust (mesh bridge with HMAC endpoints), and TypeScript (operator monitor + frontend dashboards).
